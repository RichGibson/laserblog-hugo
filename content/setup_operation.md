# Setup

- Run 220 power for Laser, Chiller, and air compressor
- Set up chiller. I believe, and YouTube agrees, that input goes to 
  laser output, and output goes to laser input, emergency wire to laser, connect to power. 
- Air compressor connect to laser
- connect laser ground wire
- Setup up computer, connect to the USB cable coming from the laser

That may actually be it? Somehow?


# Operation

- Check water cooler level, turn on chiller. It doen't want to operate above 86 or under 50 degrees.
- Turn on Air compressor
- Turn on Laser
  (there is a switch in the back by the power cord, it should be left on, 
  turn on with the key. If necessary pull up the emergency stop)
- Turn on the computer...
