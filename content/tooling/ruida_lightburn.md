Misc notes on using the Ruida controller and Lightburn

## Fast/Slow on the Wireless Handle

The button in the middle of the motion arrows is labeled 'fast/slow'
But it didn't work.

Going into Lightburn Machine Settings under Miscellaneous there is a 
setting 'enable wireless panel speed shift' and a fast and slow speed.

![Lightburn machine settings](/images/tooling/fast_slow.png)

## Enable homing

Under vendor settings for X and Y, disabled this because I don't have
functional limit switches.
