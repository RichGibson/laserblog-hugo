![Computer works!](/images/computer_works.jpg)

The computer works! I had a pile of e waste, including lots of old cables, in the garage.
And as part of 'operation make things suck less' I bagged it up and staged it to go elsewhere.
But then, I needed a monitor cable. The monitor supported old school video and DVI, and the computer 
had HDMI and DVI. So DVI it was! Except, I didn't have a DVI cable in my box of monitor cables.

But I did have several in the bags of e waste!

The wrong reaction to that is 'I will never get rid of anything ever again.'

And then the wireless keyboard and mouse didn't work, but that was easy, just needed new batteries.
