You can press the laser button on the control panel to turn the laser on
and then press the direction buttons. This lets you cut straight lines,
like when the laser doesn't want to cut for some reason, but it engraved
something that you like.

You need to lift up on the direction buttons because after a few seconds
the laser shifts to fast move mode.
