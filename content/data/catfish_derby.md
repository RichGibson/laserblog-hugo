[The Clearlake Oaks - Glennhaven Catfish Derby](https://www.clearlakeoaks.org/derby)
has been held annually for over forty years.

The event is held on approximately the same dates in May, and arguably attracts 
the most effective catchers of Catfish, so I think that the time series data reflects
something real.

But I'm not sure what, exactly, it reflects!

I got a copy of the data on fish that have been weighed in going back to 2002.
And made some graphs.
