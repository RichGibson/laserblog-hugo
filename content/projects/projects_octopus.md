![An Octopus in a Tuxedo](/images/octopus/octopus_tuxedo_start.jpg)

How to I engrave this?

- magic wand select the almost-white and fill it with true white.
  (convert transparent to white)

- remove the arc of black dots, I don't know what that might have
been, but I don't like it.

- Edited out details I didn't like. Saved it in gimp XCF

- Convert to Grayscale Image->Mode->Grayscale

- And then convert that back to RGB Image->Mode->RGB

- Save it as a JPG

![An Octopus in a Tuxedo-Engrave Try 1](/images/octopus/octopus_tuxedo_engrave_1.png)

Cast acrylic is better for engraving than extruded. I used some 4mm smoke 
colored cast acrylic from my old days in the Tap Plastics off cut bin.


![Finn watching Octopus etch](/images/octopus/octopus_finn_laser.jpg)

Engrave: speed 300 power 40
Cut: speed 20 power 85, 3 times.

The paper barely had ghost marks. I think going faster and doing more passes 
is helpful.
