I saw a design for a holder for coffee pods, and I bought the design off of
Etsy (their store is apparently now 'taking a break', so no link).

It was a simple piece with four 'slots' for coffee pods.

I expanded on the design. I made the slots longer and added a slot, added
a shelf to hold boxes of pods, added holes for screws (the original suggested
using double sided tape to mount), and made little spacers to move the holder
out a bit from the wall.

![Nescafe parts](/images/nescafe/nescafe_layout.jpg)

I am not sure why I didn't think to use finger joints on the shelf and shelf
brackets. That would have helped with alignment!

![Nescafe at an angle](/images/nescafe/nescafe_cover.jpg)
