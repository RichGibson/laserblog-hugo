Adjusting Laser Pulse power

I am calibrating the mirrors by using tape and pulsing the laser to see
where the beam hits.

The pulse power is set in the controller, and is not affected by Lightburn.

I think.

Press the 'power' button on the controller to get this display. Initially 
MaxPower1 and MaxPower2 were set to 50%. MinPower1 and MinPower2 were at 20%.

Pulsing often caused the tape to burst into unwanted flames.

![Ruida Display](/images/projects/ruida_power.jpg)

The display says 'Press<Enter> to modify,' and that wording caused the literalist
in me some problems!

If I were writing the text it would say 'Press<Enter> to save changes.'

Use the arrows to select which power setting to change and then use the numeric
keypad to enter a new number. You can use the left arrow on the keypad to delete
numbers.

Press <enter> when everything is set correctly.

There are two power settings because some lasers have two laser tubes.
