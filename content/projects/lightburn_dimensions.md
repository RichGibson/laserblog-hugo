I get fractional measurements from my drill index guage. But how does
that map to decimal measurements?

Or, I have a dimension which is a function of more than one number.

Like a reasonable CAD program, Lightburn lets you enter an equation
for a measurement.

Like 5/32, and it makes it 0.1563

And that is fun.
