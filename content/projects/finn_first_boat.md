Finn wanted a boat. Just five pieces, and he helpfully drew a picture 
of it.

![Finn's initial design](/images/projects/finn_boat_initial.jpg)

I found a [Laser cut Noah's Ark DXF](https://3axis.co/laser-cut-noahs-ark-dxf-file/r7vmyg27/)
and downloaded it.

![Image from ther 3axis.co web site of Noah's arc model](/images/projects/noahs_arc_r7vmyg27.jpg)

Corel Draw loaded the plans as 'outlines,' at least, once I clicked 'convert outline to object'
I got something I could work with.

My wood is 5.5mm The slots are 3.175 high. So some work on the tabs and slots.

It did not actually go as well as I had hoped...

Finn wanted his little five piece boat, and I made it way too complicated. 

Note to self: try to do that less.
