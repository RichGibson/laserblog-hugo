Cutting knobs is pretty easy. Gluing them together without smearing cyanoacrylate
glue on everything is harder.

Cut the parts out. Glue the nut into the appropriate place. Glue the matching
piece on one side of that, and the smaller round piece on the other side. 

![opaque knobs](/images/projects/knobs_white.jpg)

I experimented with opaque acrylic, and that worked better in that you don't 
see the glue.

Also, practice may not make perfect. But practice makes less suck.
